Program Overview

ANM (Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology) is a 2 year undergraduate program combining theory () and practical training (lab training & clinical rotation) to prepare competent nurses to cater towards excellent patient care, adequate assistance to Doctors, community health, clinical ethics and leadership roles.
